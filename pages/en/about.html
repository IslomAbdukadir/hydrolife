About

HYDROLIFE BOTTLERS is one of the leading drinking water producers in Uzbekistan.

The main activity of HYDROLIFE BOTTLERS is the production and wholesale of bottled drinking carbonated and non-carbonated water.

Entered the drinking water market of Uzbekistan in 2003, environmentally friendly, natural, mountain water company HYDROLIFE BOTTLERS fell in love with all the inhabitants of our region, and since 2016 the products our The company is also exported to the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Highly qualified personnel, culture of extraction and multi-stage water purification, use modern equipment and the latest technologies, guarantee the preservation of all useful properties and pristine water purity Hydrolife®.

Experts noted a favorable combination of medicinal properties and taste of water Hydrolife®, and high quality HYDROLIFE BOTTLERS products are confirmed by the results of numerous examinations and a growing demand on them.

The main idea of ​​the Hydrolife® trademark is high-quality, natural, mountain, drinking water according to affordable price in Uzbekistan. The main goal of the Company HYDROLIFE BOTTLERS is to meet the needs of the population Republic Uzbekistan, clean drinking water.

It should be noted that the Company HYDROLIFE BOTTLERS has gone through the entire cycle of international certification audit, and received the international certificate ISO 9001: 2008 "Management quality" as well as the international certificate ISO 22000: 2005 "Food Management System security."
